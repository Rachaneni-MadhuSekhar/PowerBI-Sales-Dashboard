# 🍫Global Chocolate Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The Global Chocolate Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze chocolate sales performance across different products, regions, sales teams, and time periods.
This dashboard helps businesses monitor revenue, profitability, and sales trends, enabling data-driven decision-making.
The project demonstrates practical data analytics skills including data modeling, KPI design, and interactive dashboard development.

## 🎯Project Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:
Analyze sales performance of chocolate products
Identify top performing products
Evaluate salesperson performance
Understand profit contribution by region
Track sales trends over time
Build an interactive business dashboard

## 📊Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard focuses on the following business KPIs:
Sales KPIs
Total Sales Amount
Total Profit
Total Boxes Sold
Average Order Value
Performance KPIs
Profit Percentage
Sales by Product
Sales by Region
Sales by Salesperson
Time KPIs
Monthly Sales Trend
Yearly Sales Performance

## 📈Dashboard Features
1️⃣ Product Performance
Sales and profit comparison by product
Top performing chocolate products
2️⃣ Geographic Analysis
Profit contribution by country
Regional sales comparison
3️⃣ Time Analysis
Monthly sales trend visualization
Interactive Year and Month filters
4️⃣ Salesperson Analysis
Total sales by salesperson
Profit percentage comparison
5️⃣ Team Performance
Profit contribution by team
6️⃣ Product Category Distribution
Category share visualization
